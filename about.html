Our Mission


At Wake Oye, our mission is to revolutionize tea and coffee breaks by providing instant, delicious, and hygienic premix solutions through modern vending machines. We strive to bring ease, consistency, and great taste to workplaces, cafeterias, canteens, and public spaces— making every sip energizing and memorable. Our goal is to make quality tea and coffee accessible to everyone, anytime, anywhere.

Our Vision


Our Vision At Wake Oye, our vision is to become the leading name in instant tea and coffee solutions across Pakistan. We aim to make every workplace, café, and canteen a hub of refreshment and energy through innovative vending technology and premium blends.We envision a future where convenience, quality, and great taste are always within reach — revolutionizing the way people experience their tea and coffee breaks, one cup at a time.

Our Clients


We are proud to serve a growing network of businesses across Pakistan. From corporate offices and educational institutions to cafés, canteens, and mini-marts — our clients trust Wake Oye to deliver consistent, high-quality tea and coffee experiences. Their satisfaction drives our passion, and their trust fuels our mission. We're honored to be part of their daily routine.



Our Team


At Wake Oye, our Team is built on the foundation of hard work, determination, and a relentless commitment to excellence. Each member plays a crucial role, working tirelessly behind the scenes to bring you the best in tea, coffee, and customer service. From developing innovative vending solutions to ensuring quality control and customer satisfaction, our team is dedicated to making every cup perfect. We believe that true success comes from consistent effort, and it's this hard work that drives us to continuously improve and exceed expectations.
